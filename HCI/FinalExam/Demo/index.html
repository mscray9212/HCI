For this JCC template, I have decided to
demonstrate a user-friendly virtual tour.

The virtual tour will be interactive with options to navigate to different areas within the JCC,
giving users a personal interaction with the culture of Japanese living. All areas of the virtual tour
will be updated when the JCC has finished construction (with actual on-site photos). Hope you enjoy your visit!

Imperial Palace

The Japanese Cultural Center will have a walk-in model of the Imperial Palace in Tokyo. How cool is that!?

Another walk-in model of a Japanese temple will be available for all to view. Getting excited yet??

Botanical Garden

Next look at this cool stuff

Imperial Palace

Currently, the imperial palace is closed to the public except for New Year's Greeting (January 2nd) and on the Emperor's birthday (December 23). The Imperial Palace walk-in will offer a first hand experience, year-round!

Japanese Temple

This walk-in will include a main hall, lecture hall, pagoda, bell, and a gate to pass for entrance. Inside, visitors will also see some Buddhist objects that can be seen throughout Kyoto and other historically rich areas in Japan.

Cultural Garden

Similar to this Huntington Library botanical garden, the Atlanta JCC will be implementing a beautiful landscape for all visitors to enjoy. The JCC will partner with SkwiuFarms to find out which cultural plants will be best to implement in the garden.

Another JCC

This JCC is located in Chicago and should give an idea on what we are hoping to offer in Atlanta. Please feel free to browse this site and let us know if there is anything that you really want us to incorporate into YOUR JCC
