About us

A future non profit 501(c)3 organization, Skwiu Farms, is focused on enriching local communities close to the Piedmont and Mountainside of North Carolina through the creation of local cooperative markets based in high quality, free-range poultry, and organically produce grown locally in the nearby area. Skwiu Farms strictly adheres to a no synthetics in the garden policy, a non GMO policy, and we also focus on bringing foods of the native, indigenous Americans to the people in communities adjacent to our projects. Skwiu Farms implements a hands on philosophy for education and outreach, bringing the best of locally grown exotics and natives for a bio-diverse diet from our welcoming community. Feel free to contact us with any feedback!

Our website is a platform through which people can understand more about what we do, or that wish to contact our organization to arrange for accommodations to anyone that wishes to stay or visit. We hope that you will visit our links for our data, and make suggestions to any exotic plants for conservation. The links above are recorded to our Google Drive and will be updated as often as possible. Also, we will be documenting the progress and pitfalls of Skwiu Farms, so come on and join along!

 

Skwiu Farms consists of many people. From permaculture designers, artists, jack-of-all-trades, apprentices, and scientists. Our unifying force originates in our shared common interest, which is to flourish with abundant food growing nearby to ensure our healthy state and prosperity. Our projects guarantee a mutually beneficial system for the soil microbes and people, and provide carbon sequestration from the atmoshpere. We will utilize some known exotic foods, but focus on maintaining a 60% minimum on native plant species. Sections of Skwiu farms are allotted for the conservation of native plants and restoring the populations of native, and exotic, endagered plants. We expect and openly welcome scientific researchers who want to study these plants, the soil, as well as videographers or artists who want to document our experiences. We do not discriminate on gender or race; however Skwiu Farms expects, regardless of age, you will be able to find productive ways to work, teach, and share.
